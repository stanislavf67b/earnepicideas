When he saw Henry playing his heart out on the guitar, Officer Jackson decided to take a leap of faith. He immediately pulled to the side of the road and approached Henry.

Officer Jackson and Henry after turning his life around! Henry didn't even have an ID card to open a bank account, so they went together to get him one.
